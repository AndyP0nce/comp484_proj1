Wall-E

A 2008 animated science fiction film produced by Pixar Animation Studios and released by Walt Disney Pictures.

Home The Terminator Real Steel The Iron Giant Wall-E

Wall-E is set in a distant future where Earth has been abandoned by humans due to excessive waste and pollution. The story follows Wall-E, a small waste-collecting robot, who embarks on a space journey that ultimately decides the fate of mankind.
