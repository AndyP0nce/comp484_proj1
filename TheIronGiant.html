The Iron Giant

A 1999 animated science fiction film directed by Brad Bird and produced by Warner Bros. Animation.

Home The Terminator Real Steel The Iron Giant Wall-E

The Iron Giant is set during the Cold War era and follows a young boy named Hogarth Hughes who discovers a giant robot from outer space. As Hogarth befriends the robot, they must evade government agents and a paranoid military while learning about friendship, bravery, and the potential for both destruction and redemption.
