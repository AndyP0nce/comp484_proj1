Real Steel

A 2011 science fiction sports film directed by Shawn Levy and starring Hugh Jackman.

Real Steel is set in the near future where robot boxing is a popular sport. The story follows Charlie Kenton, a former boxer, and his estranged son, Max, as they build and train a robot named Atom to compete in the robot boxing league.
