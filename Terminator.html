The Terminator

A 1984 science fiction film directed by James Cameron and starring Arnold Schwarzenegger.

Home The Terminator Real Steel The Iron Giant Wall-E

The Terminator is set in a dystopian future where an artificial intelligence system called Skynet becomes self-aware and initiates a nuclear apocalypse. The story follows a cyborg assassin, the Terminator, sent back in time to kill Sarah Connor, whose unborn son will lead the human resistance against Skynet.
